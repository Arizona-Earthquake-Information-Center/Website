Welcome to the Arizona Earthquake Information Center (AEIC) website

The purpose of the AEIC is to collect and distribute information on Arizona earthquakes, and to conduct research on them. We are located in the Geology building (Frier Hall) on the campus of Northern Arizona University. Feel free to explore the pages within via the various links on the left side of the screen.
