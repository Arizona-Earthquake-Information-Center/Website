AEIC-Bulletin list server

What:  Staff of the AEIC will post information about earthquakes in Arizona (Earthquake bulletins, news, information, alerts, updates) when new information is available. 

 

  • Subscribing To The List.

 

If you do choose to subscribe to this list, you can subscribe at any time by sending email to:

 LISTSERV@LISTS.NAU.EDU

with a single line of text in the body of the message that says:

 SUBSCRIBE AEIC-BULLETIN Your_first_name Your_last_name

 

Example:

   To: LISTSERV@LISTS.NAU.EDU

   Subj:

   SUBSCRIBE AEIC-BULLETIN Jane Doe

 

  • Unsubscribing From The List.

 

If you wish to unsubscribe from this mailing list, please send email to:

  LISTSERV@LISTS.NAU.EDU

with a single line of text in the body of the message that says:

  SIGNOFF AEIC-BULLETIN

        or

  UNSUBSCRIBE AEIC-BULLETIN

 

 

Example:

 

   To: LISTSERV@LISTS.NAU.EDU

   Subj:

   UNSUBSCRIBE AEIC-BULLETIN
