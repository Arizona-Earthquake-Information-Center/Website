September 3, 2009

FREDONIA EARTHQUAKE

 

A magnitude 3.0 earthquake struck near Fredonia, Arizona last night at 11:00 p.m. local time.  A smaller earthquake occurred in the same area at 1:59 a.m. local time the previous night.  As of this bulletin, no felt or damage reports have been received at the center. 

The earthquake data are as follows:

  • Magnitude (ML) 3.0, Thursday, September 3, 2009 11:00 p.m. MST 36.93N x 112.51W
