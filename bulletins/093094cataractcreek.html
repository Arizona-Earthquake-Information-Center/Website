September 30, 1994

CATARACT CREEK

 

Two small northern Arizona earthquakes have occurred during the past week. The epicenters are located approximately 30 miles northwest of Flagstaff, AZ. About 6 miles northwest of Kendrick Peak.

The recent earthquakes occurred within the Cataract Creek Fault System. An earthquake sequence in April 1003, including magnitudes 4.9 and 5.4 events, occurred along the same fault system about 50 miles northwest of Flagstaff in the region of Valle, Arizona

The data are as follows:

  • Friday, September 23, 1994 ML 2.6 5:46pm 35.46N x 111.99W
  • Thursday, September 29, 1994 ML 3.0 4:21am 35.46N x 111.99W
