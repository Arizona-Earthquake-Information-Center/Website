Home > Bulletins > Mexican earthquake felt in Yuma, AZ.

September 19, 2009

 

A magnitude 5.1 earthquake occurred at 3:55:17 MST on September 19, 2009.  The earthquake was located 43 mile southwest of Yuma, Arizona near the Mexican town of Delta.  Weak shaking was reported by residents of Yuma, Arizona.  Details of the earthquake can be found at the National Earthquake Information Center website.
